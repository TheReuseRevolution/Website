Love-a-Mug

Mug off single-use cups and love a reusable mug.

Here’s the tea:

  • Single-use cups contain plastic lining and PFAS forever chemicals

Harming:

  • The environment: pollution in the landscape releasing GHG over thousands of years
  • Human health: hormone disruptors and microplastics

Australia: 1.5 billion single-use coffee cups a year … that’s thousands every second.

Our pollution solution? A hug in a mug!

We give mugs a longer life, saving op shop donations from landfill, giving them back to the community.

We froth sustainability – so thanks a latte for helping people and the planet one mug at a time.

CO₂e Savings Tracker

Every time you use a second-hand mug instead of a disposable coffee cup, you avoid approximately 0.025 kg CO₂e.

Our community has already avoided 50,000 disposable cups, saving about 1.25 tonnes CO₂e.

About Us – Social Enterprise

We’re a social enterprise dedicated to reducing single-use waste and changing community habits. Our mission is to inspire reuse, cut down waste, and promote a culture of slowing down to appreciate life’s simple pleasures.

Blog – Tips for Reducing Single-Use

We share tips and stories to help you avoid single-use items, embrace reusables, and live more sustainably. Slow down, drink your coffee sitting down, and take 5 minutes to appreciate the moment.
